Our Constituents

Shrubs

Medicinal shrubs are plants used for their therapeutic properties, offering remedies for various ailments. These shrubs often provide essential oils, compounds, and extracts that are integral to traditional and modern herbal medicine.

Herbs

Medicinal herbs are plants uused for their healing properties, often in the form of teas, tinctures or extracts, These herbs contain active compounds that can help alleviate symptoms and support overall health.
